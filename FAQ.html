Frequently Asked Questions

What is CBD?

CBD, short for cannabidiol, is one of the naturally occurring (& most prevalent) cannabinoids created by the hemp plant. It’s effects are non-psychoactive.

Besides CBD, what other natural compounds can be found within Daily Dose Full Spectrum products?

Beyond only CBD, Full Spectrum products contain many other cannabinoids from the hemp plant including naturally occurring terpenes, essential vitamins, fatty acids, proteins, & more. CBC, CBG, CBDV, & trace amounts of Delta-9 THC can all be found within our craft oils. This is where the term “entourage effect” comes into play – it’s because of the synergistic relationship between all these cannabinoids & the body’s Endocannabinoid System that CBD can provide its user an optimal healing experience. It is our firm belief that it is because of the inclusion of every naturally occurring cannabinoid that our products can offer the best healing possible.

Is CBD legal?

Yes! CBD & hemp-derived products are legal in all 50 states.

Will CBD make me high?

Daily Dose CBD Inc.’s products are non-psychoactive. Although THC is present in our Full Spectrum products, we assure you that the amounts are well below the legal limit of .3% by weight. Every one of our products is third-party lab tested to maintain the highest standard of quality and legality.

Will I pass a drug test?

We cannot make any claims on whether or not any of our products will show up on a drug test. Our products are crafted from a Full Spectrum CBD oil & do contain less than 0.3% THC by weight. We are not legally able to make any recommendations or guarantees regarding drug tests while using our products. If that is a concern, we would recommend not consuming any Full Spectrum CBD products and/or doing some further research at www.ProjectCBD.org before making the decision to include it in your regimen.
